About Us

This website was built by one person who simply enjoys flowers. Since I was named after a flower (zinnias!), I've liked learning more about them. Of course flowers are beautiful, but I was always more interested in the meanings behind flowers and what secret messages they could send. However, researching these meanings was often times laborious and difficult because of the lack of comprehensive sites and the ads on the sites that do exist. Thus this webpage was created to consolidate my research.

My family owns a small garden and so in the summer we are always thinking about what to grow to make the most use out of the growing season. Thus, knowing what flowers are the best to grow and how to grow them became important if I wanted to cultivate my own batch of garden flowers.
